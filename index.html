INTRODUCTION

NATIONAL SYMPOSIUM of Institutional Waste Water Recycling, A Symposium organized by the Rajabazar Science College Students’ Union, to find solutions so that together we can battle the challenge of water scarcity which has resulted due to excessive wastage of water. Come join us in making this a success. It would not only provide a platform to innovative minds but also contribute to society in a way it really needs. Together let’s leave behind a legacy of Innovation, Excellence and Awareness.



ABOUT US

The University of Calcutta truly lives up to its motto, "Advancement of Learning", and its students work day and night, unified together to serve its cause. The young minds actively join hands forming the Rajabazar Science College Students' Union, which works with betterment of the University as its primary goal.

Organising many events throughout the widespread campuses across the city, bringing everyone together for noble pursuits, the Students' Union (Rajabazar Science College) promotes harmony through various cultural activities like Rangotsav( the celebration of the festival of colours) and the annual cultural fest. While on one hand they organise a Saraswati Puja (at Nano Campus), however, the Science College is witness to one of the grandest iftar gatherings during Ramadan. Various other activities include annual picnics, cricket tournaments, football tournaments all year round. Eminent days like the independence day, teachers' day and international mother language day are celebrated with great pride by the Students' Union. A technical fest like Regalo Technologia is to be organised by them, aided by the University, providing students across colleges a platform to sharpen their technological skills and understanding. Social welfare activities like health checkup camps and blood donation camps are set up in regular intervals. The enthused spirit of the young hearts, working for their own Students' Union, has made it possible to extend its reach beyond international boundaries. Proud moments of international visit along with a panorama of all other activities are exhibited in the annual magazine.

Encouraging new ideas has always been a key programme of the students' union and hence it has come together again to organise the 1st edition of the National Symposium on Institutional Waste Water Recycling on the 12th of September, 2019. In this hour of need, when a major part of the country is battling the challenge of water scarcity, a Symposium like this will encourage several creative ideas to recycle the waste water produced from an institution, economically and effectively. We believe it will be a big step towards spreading more awareness about the importance of water resource conservation.

Interactive sessions with dignitaries from the industries will be enlightening. A presentation challenge inviting participation from students and scholars alike, exhibiting their creative thinking is the key event. Other parallel competitions will include photography and poster making. We expect enthusiastic response and participation from students all over the city.
